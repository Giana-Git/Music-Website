About Giana關於 Giana

The Artist藝術家

Giana Yeung is a Southampton-based vocalist and guzheng artist working across classical, musical theatre and pop traditions. Trained in vocal performance at the University of Southampton and in guzheng at the Central Conservatory of Music, she brings two musical worlds — Western vocal artistry and the 2,500-year-old Chinese zither — into one teaching studio.

Giana Yeung 是駐修咸頓的歌唱家及古箏演奏者，涉獵古典、音樂劇及流行等多種風格。她於修咸頓大學修讀聲樂演唱，並持有中央音樂學院古箏文憑，將西方聲樂藝術與擁有二千五百年歷史的中國箏樂融匯於同一個教學空間。

Her performing life began early: during her school years she took top-three placings in the girls' solo singing classes of the Hong Kong Schools Music Festival on multiple occasions. She has since performed at more than fifty venues and events across London, the South of England and Hong Kong, and in 2023 she won the Southampton Music and Drama Festival in both the Adult Songs from the Shows and Adult Vocal Recital classes.

她的演出生涯始於求學時期，曾多次於香港校際音樂節女聲獨唱項目取得三甲成績。此後，她於倫敦、英國南部及香港超過五十個場地與活動中演出，並於 2023 年修咸頓音樂及戲劇節（Southampton Music and Drama Festival）成人組別 Songs from the Shows 及 Vocal Recital 兩個項目中雙雙奪冠。

As a teacher, Giana believes every voice — and every pair of hands — deserves patient, personal guidance. Lessons are structured but warm: technique is built step by step, repertoire is chosen with the student, and progress is celebrated whether the goal is a distinction at Grade 8 or simply the joy of making music.

作為導師，Giana 相信每一把聲音、每一雙手都值得耐心而個人化的引導。課堂結構清晰而氣氛溫暖：技巧逐步建立，曲目與學生共同挑選；無論目標是八級優異成績，還是純粹享受音樂的快樂，每一點進步都值得肯定。

What I Teach教授科目

The Teacher課程

Every singing lesson includes warm-up, technique, repertoire, and theory & aural — tailored to your goals. 每堂唱歌課均包括熱身、技巧、曲目及樂理與聽力訓練，並按你的目標度身安排。

I.

Classical Vocal古典聲樂

Healthy classical technique, breath support, and art song & aria repertoire. 建立健康的古典發聲技巧與氣息支持，研習藝術歌曲及詠嘆調。

  • Beginner to Grade 8初學至八級
  • ABRSM / Trinity exam preparationABRSM / Trinity 考級準備
II.

Musical Theatre音樂劇唱腔

Belt and legit styles, acting through song, and audition-ready performance. 教授 Belt 與傳統唱法，以歌傳情，並為試鏡演出作好準備。

  • Song interpretation & storytelling歌曲演繹與敘事

III.

Pop Singing流行唱歌

Contemporary vocal style, mic technique, riffs and building your own sound. 掌握現代流行唱腔、咪高風技巧與轉音，發展屬於自己的聲音。

The Chinese zither — from your very first notes to intermediate repertoire. 由第一個音開始，循序漸進學習古箏，直至中級曲目。

Frequently Asked常見問題

Questions問答
Do I need my own guzheng?學古箏需要自備樂器嗎？

Not to begin — you can learn on my instrument during lessons at my home. As you progress, I'll happily advise on buying or renting an instrument suited to your level and budget. 初學階段不需要 — 於本人寓所上課時可使用我的古箏。隨著程度提升，我會就購買或租借合適樂器提供建議。

What ages do you teach?收生年齡有限制嗎？

All ages are welcome, from young children to adults. Under 12s receive 10% off all lessons, and complete beginners are always welcome — no experience needed. 任何年齡均歡迎，由小童到成人。12 歲以下所有課程 9 折，完全零基礎的初學者亦非常歡迎。

Can you prepare me for exams or auditions?可以幫我準備考級或試鏡嗎？

Yes — singing lessons cover exam preparation up to Grade 8, including theory and aural, and I coach musical theatre auditions. Guzheng lessons prepare students up to Grade 6. 可以 — 唱歌課程涵蓋至八級考試準備，包括樂理及聽力訓練，並提供音樂劇試鏡指導。古箏課程可準備至六級。

Can I take lessons online?可以網上上課嗎？

Singing lessons are available online worldwide over Google Meet, charged in GBP. Guzheng is taught in person only, so hands-on technique gets the attention it needs. 唱歌課程可透過 Google Meet 網上進行，全球適用，以英鎊收費。古箏課程只限面授，以確保手法技巧得到細緻指導。

What happens in a taster lesson?體驗課包括甚麼？

A relaxed 30 minutes (£20) where we chat about your goals, try some singing or playing, and I assess your current level — then I'll recommend a plan that fits you. 輕鬆的 30 分鐘（£20）：我們會傾談你的目標，試唱或試彈，我會評估你目前的程度，然後建議最適合你的學習計劃。
