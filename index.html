Giana Yeung is a Southampton-based vocalist and guzheng artist working across classical, musical theatre and pop traditions. Trained in vocal performance at the University of Southampton and in guzheng at the Central Conservatory of Music, she brings two musical worlds — Western vocal artistry and the 2,500-year-old Chinese zither — into one teaching studio.

Giana Yeung 是駐修咸頓的歌唱家及古箏演奏者，涉獵古典、音樂劇及流行等多種風格。她於修咸頓大學修讀聲樂演唱，並持有中央音樂學院古箏文憑，將西方聲樂藝術與擁有二千五百年歷史的中國箏樂融匯於同一個教學空間。

Questions常見問題

Frequently Asked常見問題

Do I need my own guzheng?學古箏需要自備樂器嗎？

Not to begin — you can learn on my instrument during lessons at my home. As you progress, I'll happily advise on buying or renting an instrument suited to your level and budget. 初學階段不需要 — 於本人寓所上課時可使用我的古箏。隨著程度提升，我會就購買或租借合適樂器提供建議。

What ages do you teach?收生年齡有限制嗎？

All ages are welcome, from young children to adults. Under 12s receive 10% off all lessons, and complete beginners are always welcome — no experience needed. 任何年齡均歡迎，由小童到成人。12 歲以下所有課程 9 折，完全零基礎的初學者亦非常歡迎。

Can you prepare me for exams or auditions?可以幫我準備考級或試鏡嗎？

Yes — singing lessons cover exam preparation up to Grade 8, including theory and aural, and I coach musical theatre auditions. Guzheng lessons prepare students up to Grade 6. 可以 — 唱歌課程涵蓋至八級考試準備，包括樂理及聽力訓練，並提供音樂劇試鏡指導。古箏課程可準備至六級。

Can I take lessons online?可以網上上課嗎？

Singing lessons are available online worldwide over Google Meet, charged in GBP. Guzheng is taught in person only, so hands-on technique gets the attention it needs. 唱歌課程可透過 Google Meet 網上進行，全球適用，以英鎊收費。古箏課程只限面授，以確保手法技巧得到細緻指導。

What happens in a taster lesson?體驗課包括甚麼？

A relaxed 30 minutes (£20) where we chat about your goals, try some singing or playing, and I assess your current level — then I'll recommend a plan that fits you. 輕鬆的 30 分鐘（£20）：我們會傾談你的目標，試唱或試彈，我會評估你目前的程度，然後建議最適合你的學習計劃。
